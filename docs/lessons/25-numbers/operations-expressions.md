# 25.4. Arithmetic Operations and Numerical Expressions

|    Operation   |   Word(s)  |                Example                |          Translation          |
|:--------------:|:----------:|:-------------------------------------:|:-----------------------------:|
|   Addition +   |    Plus    |      Uno plus quattro èst cinque.     |     One plus four is five.    |
|   Subtraction  |    Menos   |       Dèce menos tres èst sèpte.      |   Ten minus three is seven.   |
| Multiplication |    Veces   |      Doos veces cinque èst dèce.      |     Two times five is ten.    |
|    Division    | Diviso per | Quaranta divisa per dèce èst quattro. | Forty divided by ten is four. |
